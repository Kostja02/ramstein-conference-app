Help section

The functions explained below are tested with firefox and Chrome, both on Android and MacOS. They should work on other browsers too, but no guarantee for that :)

PS: If you tested it on Safari and/or Edge, let me know!

Push-Notifications

On the first load, a dialog box at the bottom asks you for enabling push-notifications. If you accidentally clicked "Deny", you can re-enable them inside the site-settings (click on the lock left of the URL).

Add to homescreen

You should see a "Add to Homescreen" Bar in mobile browsers. By clicking on it, you can (surprise) add the app to your homescreen or list of apps. It should then also work offline.
